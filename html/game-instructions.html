How To Play

Players take turns placing their pieces, one per turn, into the center area.

RULES FOR PLAYING PIECES
• Once a piece is placed, it cannot be moved.
• Keep placing pieces until someone gets an Otrio!
WINNING
Be the first one to get three of your pieces in a row in one of three ways:

Three of the same sized pieces, big medium or small in a row
(in any direction vertically, horizontally or diagonally)

Three concentric pieces in any one space.

Three pieces in ascending or descending order
(in any direction vertically horizontally or diagonally)
